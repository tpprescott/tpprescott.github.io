Plan in advance

Plan in advance

Why? To optimise remaining time, reduce feelings of stress, and avoid time-wasting.

[And it's required for transfer and confirmation.]

Should provide for the unexpected.

Devise and agree with supervisor, and review every few weeks.

No definitive way to plan - mind-maps, timelines.

Make your priorities clear

Clarify your priorities

Competing priorities: concluding experiments/analysis for a final chapter or paper; mandatory admin tasks; preparing for the next steps of your career.

It is important to be able to say "no" to being offered responsibility for new projects or students. And it's difficult to turn down exciting opportunities.

Stick to the plan!

"The truth can wait"

"The truth can wait"

The perfect doctoral thesis does not exist: do not try to do everything before submitting.

A common mistake is to go back to the lab instead of getting started with the results chapters of the thesis.

To postpone writing will cause delays and not necessarily improve the thesis.

Exploit synergies

Exploit synergies

You are doing a lot of work in your thesis, so use it to your advantage.

Literature review --> Published review article.

Thesis intro/conclusion --> Fellowship proposal.

Talks/visits --> Viva/interview practice.

Enlist support

Get familiar with the software

Get familiar with the software

LaTeX: Tracking changes/collaboration, personalise macros and environments, useful packages.

Graphics design: Plotly, Inkscape, GIMP.

Slides: Powerpoint, Beamer, Reveal.js.

Reference management: Endnote, Mendeley, Jabref.

Version control: Git, Subversion, Bazaar.

Backups: Maths Institute, Google Drive, Onedrive, Dropbox.

Do the admin

Know Oxford's procedures and regulations

Prepare and list all required documentation.

Maintain a good working relationship with someone who can help you navigate the bureaucratic process.

Don't just borrow and adapt previously completed forms, as forms change.

Be aware of Maths/Oxford's requirements for your thesis submission: deadlines, copy number, format, font size, binding, supporting documents.

Do the admin early, while stress levels are low and enthusiasm high!

Pay attention to your career

Pay attention to your career

For those who are determined to have an academic career, a strategic research plan is crucial.

For those who are unsure, a viable alternative career plan is equally important.

Leave on good terms
