The Bayesian computation will depend on the model: for a given value of θ, the probability of seeing H HEADS and T TAILS is the Binomial probability with H+T trials and success probability θ.

Under this model, all of the possible likelihoods LH,T(θ)=p(H,T | θ) are functions of θ for fixed values of the data, H and T, and can all easily be calculated using the probability density function of the Binomial distribution.
